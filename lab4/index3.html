List for Task 1

  1. Find a reliable recipe.
  2. Gather all the ingredients.
  3. Mix the dough ingredients together by hand or use a hand-held or stand mixer.
    • Whisk the warm water, yeast, and granulated sugar together.
    • Add the olive oil, salt, and flour.
  4. Knead by hand or beat the dough with your mixer for 2 minutes.
  5. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface.
    • With lightly floured hands, knead the dough for 3-4 minutes.
  6. Place dough into a greased mixing bowl.
    • cover tightly, and set aside to rise for about 90 minutes or overnight in the refrigerator.
  7. Punch down risen dough to release air bubbles. Divide in 2
  8. Roll dough out into a 12-inch circle.
    • Sprinkle flour on a clean work surface and use a rolling pin.
  9. Using lightly floured hands, stretch and flatten the disc onto the pan.
  10. Preheat the oven to 475°F (246°C). Allow it to heat for at least 15-20 minutes as you shape the pizza
  11. Top with favorite pizza toppings.
  12. Bake for 15 minutes or until the bottom crust is golden brown.
